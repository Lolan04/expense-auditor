TRAVEL & EXPENSE POLICY — COMPANY POLICY MANUAL v2.1

SECTION 2.1 — BUSINESS JUSTIFICATION
All expense claims must include a clear and specific business justification.
Vague descriptions such as "meeting", "misc", or "general" are not acceptable.
Minimum description length is 20 characters.

SECTION 3.1 — MEAL LIMITS BY LOCATION
New York: Meals limit is $75 per claim.
London: Meals limit is £40 per claim.
Mumbai: Meals limit is INR 1200 per claim.
Chicago: Meals limit is $60 per claim.
Default cities: Meals limit is $50 per claim.

SECTION 3.2 — TRANSPORT LIMITS BY LOCATION
New York: Transport limit is $50 per claim.
London: Transport limit is £35 per claim.
Mumbai: Transport limit is INR 800 per claim.
Chicago: Transport limit is $45 per claim.
Default cities: Transport limit is $30 per claim.

SECTION 3.3 — LODGING LIMITS BY LOCATION
New York: Lodging limit is $250 per night.
London: Lodging limit is £200 per night.
Mumbai: Lodging limit is INR 5000 per night.
Chicago: Lodging limit is $220 per night.
Default cities: Lodging limit is $150 per night.

SECTION 3.4 — CONFERENCE AND TRAINING
New York: Conference limit is $500 per event.
London: Conference limit is £400 per event.
Mumbai: Conference limit is INR 8000 per event.
Default cities: Conference limit is $300 per event.

SECTION 4.1 — CLOSE TO LIMIT WARNING
Any claim within 15% of the applicable spending limit must be flagged
for senior review before reimbursement is processed.

SECTION 4.2 — PROHIBITED EXPENSES
The following are strictly non-reimbursable under any circumstances:
Alcohol, bar tabs, nightclub entry, casino expenses, personal shopping,
spa treatments, gym memberships, vacation expenses, holiday travel,
party expenses, and personal entertainment of any kind.

SECTION 5.1 — RECEIPT REQUIREMENTS
All claims above $25 must be accompanied by an original receipt.
Receipts must show merchant name, date, itemized costs, and total amount.

SECTION 5.2 — CURRENCY
All claims must be submitted in the currency of the country of expenditure.
Foreign currency claims will be converted at the rate on the date of expense.

SECTION 5.3 — WEEKEND AND HOLIDAY EXPENSES
Expenses incurred on weekends (Saturday and Sunday) or public holidays
require prior written approval from the department head.
Without prior approval, weekend claims will be flagged for review.

SECTION 6.1 — APPROVAL WORKFLOW
Claims under the spending limit: Auto-approved by system.
Claims flagged by system: Require finance team review within 3 business days.
Claims rejected by system: Employee must resubmit with corrections.

SECTION 6.2 — HUMAN OVERRIDE
Finance auditors may override any AI decision with a written justification.
All overrides are logged for compliance audit purposes.

SECTION 7.1 — REIMBURSEMENT TIMELINE
Approved claims are reimbursed within 5 business days.
Flagged claims may take up to 10 business days pending review.
